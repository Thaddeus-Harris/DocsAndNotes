## Theorem 
- Every finite abelian [[Group]] is [isomorphic](homomorphism) to a product of cyclic groups of prime power order. Explicitly, if G is a finite abelian group of order n, we have $$G \cong \prod \mathbb{Z}_{p_{i}^{k_{i}}}$$
- where the $p_i$ are not necessarily distinct primes and $\prod p_i^{k_i} = n$
