## Def
- Let G be a group and $S^1$ be the unit circle $$S^1 = \{x \in \mathbb{C} : |x| = 1\}$$ under multiplication. A [[character]] of G is a [group homomorphism](homomorphism.md) $G \to S^1$ (When there is a topology on the [[Group]], [[character]]s must be [[continuous]] too. 
## Prop
- If G and H are finite abelian [[Group]]s, $\hat{G \times H} \cong \hat{G}\times \hat H$
